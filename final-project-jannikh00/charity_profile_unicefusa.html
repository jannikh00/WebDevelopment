UNICEF USA

  • Founded: 1947
  • Location: New York, NY
  • Category: Children
  • Website-Link: UNICEF USA
    Description:

UNICEF USA shares a unified vision with UNICEF: a world where every child's rights are upheld, ensuring their well-being and enabling them to thrive. Operating in over 190 countries and territories, UNICEF focuses on safeguarding and enhancing the lives of children globally, with a particular emphasis on those facing the greatest vulnerabilities.

UNICEF USA actively supports UNICEF's global mission by mobilizing the American public to champion the cause of the world's most marginalized children. As one of 33 National Committees dedicated to advocating for children worldwide, UNICEF USA plays a vital role in driving positive change.

Through initiatives like the Impact Fund for Children, UNICEF USA provides avenues for socially conscious investors to contribute to the survival and development of children. Additionally, its Advocacy team, based in Washington, D.C., tirelessly advocates for policies and budgets that prioritize children's well-being, urging decision-makers across sectors to take action.
