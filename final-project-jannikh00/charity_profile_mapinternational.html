MAP International

  • Founded: 1954
  • Location: Brunswick, GA
  • Category: Health
  • Website-Link: MAP International
    Description:

MAP International, a renowned Christian nonprofit organization, endeavors to distribute vital medicine and health supplies to underserved communities globally. With a mission rooted in the compassionate teachings of Jesus, as reflected in Matthew 25:36, MAP serves individuals regardless of religious affiliation, gender, race, nationality, or ethnicity. Its aim is to foster healthier and more fulfilling lives for all.

Your support is instrumental in advancing MAP International's noble cause. Whether through financial contributions or volunteerism, each gesture of generosity holds the power to effect positive change. Together, let us join hands in advocating for life-saving interventions and uplifting communities worldwide.
